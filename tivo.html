First Setup

Connecting to Wi-Fi

You will need to connect the Tivo to your home wi-fi network. To do this, at the main menu with all the apps on it, press and hold down the OK button on an app, then go to Android settings, and then to "Network & Internet". Then choose your Wi-Fi network and login. Once connected to Wi-Fi, all of your apps should work, except S4U, which needs another step.

Unlocking the S4U App

The S4U app is locked to your location, so the accounts can't be shared. You will need to send your IP address to me so that I can link it to your account, which I can do remotely. The easiest way to get your IP Address is to open the S4U app. It will show on the top left "No Access | 123.123.123.123" where the set of numbers is your IP address. Send that IP to me somehow and I will link it to your account. Once that is done, you can go to Manual Refresh and it will load all of the sports. This only needs to be done once, unless you change Internet providers.
